Marcus Francisco

French Horn • Voice • Ensemble Performer

On Stage

Music has run alongside my engineering career as a parallel discipline — not a weekend hobby, but a serious performance practice built over more than a decade. I began on French Horn under private instruction in high school and continued at the collegiate level, while also developing my voice in choral and a cappella settings. I have performed with orchestras, concert bands, choirs, and chamber ensembles in the Western New York region and have held music leadership roles with student ensembles at the University at Buffalo.

The same values that make a reliable engineer — precise timing, active listening, and the ability to hold your part steady while tracking the whole system — are what an ensemble demands from every chair. The two careers have always informed each other.

Competitions & Recognition

Competitive music selection processes at the county, regional, and state levels have been a consistent part of my musical biography. These auditions and composition submissions are evaluated by professional panels and represent the most selective music opportunities available in New York State.

  • Conference All-State — Performer (Multiple Years)
    Selected multiple times as a French Horn performer at the New York State Conference All-State festival — among the most competitive music honor ensembles in the state.
  • Conference All-State — Composer (Multiple Years)
    Original compositions selected for performance at the All-State level multiple times. Simultaneous recognition as performer and composer in the same festival cycle is uncommon.
  • New York State Bandmasters Association (NYSBDA)
    Performed in NYSBDA events under the direction of Dr. Stephen Shewan, alongside the most accomplished wind band musicians in New York State.
  • All Erie & All County Festivals
    Selected for county- and regional-level honor ensembles as stepping stones to state recognition, conducted by area music educators.
  • New York State All-State Music Festival (NYSSMA)
    Performed with the All-State ensemble at the NYSSMA All-State festival.
