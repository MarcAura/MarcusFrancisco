Background

Hi, I'm Marcus Francisco, a passionate Electrical Engineering student with a strong interest in advancing technology through innovation and research. I aim to contribute to the future of smart electronics and sustainable solutions.

Education

I am currently pursuing a Master's degree in Electrical Engineering at the University at Buffalo. My coursework focuses on advanced circuit design, wireless communications, and embedded systems, providing me with the skills to excel in the field of smart technologies.

Interests

Beyond academics, I am deeply interested in sustainable technology, renewable energy solutions, and the integration of IoT devices in daily life. I am also an avid music lover, playing French horn in professional ensembles and participating in local music events.

My ultimate career goal is to contribute to the development of cutting-edge technology in the field of Flexible Hybrid Electronics (FHE) and IoT devices. I aim to work in a research and development environment where I can combine my passion for electronics and sustainability to bring transformative innovations to life.
